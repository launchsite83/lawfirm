About the Firm

XYZ Law Group

Founded in 2003 by Daniel R. Voss, XYZ Law Group has spent over two decades building a reputation for uncompromising legal excellence across Houston and the greater Texas Gulf Coast. We combine deep statutory knowledge with aggressive courtroom advocacy to deliver outcomes that protect what matters most to our clients.

Our Counsel

Meet the Attorneys

Daniel R. Voss

Managing Partner

Daniel founded XYZ Law Group in 2003 after a decade as a defense-side insurance litigator. That background gives him a rare edge: he knows precisely how carriers build their cases — and exactly where they're vulnerable. He has tried over 80 cases to verdict across Texas state and federal courts.

Catherine L. Mercer

Senior Partner
